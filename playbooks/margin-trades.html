02 Trade Type Hierarchy

Setups are listed in strict order of preference. Always exhaust higher-ranked candidates before considering a lower one. If two qualifying ideas compete for the same slot, the higher-ranked setup wins.

MOST PREFERRED
1

PEMA + VCP

Post-Earnings momentum aligned with a Volatility Contraction Pattern. The highest-conviction confluence — fundamental catalyst plus tight technical base.

2

PEMA

Standalone Post-Earnings Momentum. Fundamental thrust without the tight technical base — still a high-quality, catalyst-driven setup.

3

R4 Breakout

Stage-2 trend continuation breakout from a 4-week+ consolidation. Pure technical setup — requires clean structure and volume confirmation.

4

HM

High-Momentum continuation. Riding established leadership — lower base quality than R4, requires tighter stops and faster reaction.

LAST RESORT
5

MR Long

Mean-Reversion long. Counter-trend bounce from oversold conditions. Use only when no momentum setups qualify and only on fundamentally strong names.

04 Capital & Leverage

The book funds ₹40L of equity exposure with ₹14L of own capital — the rest is broker-funded margin (MTF). Understand the implied leverage before sizing up.

Capital Stack

Total Margin Deployed ₹40L
Own Capital Deployed ₹14L
Broker Funding ₹26L
Implied Leverage ≈ 2.86×

What Leverage Means

  • ➜ A 3.5% drawdown on the ₹40L book is a 10% drawdown on the ₹14L capital.
  • ➜ Carry cost (broker interest on ₹26L) eats P&L every day a position is open — favour faster-moving setups.
  • ➜ Stops are non-negotiable. Leverage cuts both ways and a slow exit compounds losses on borrowed capital.

05 Discipline Rules

⭐ One-Line Desk Rule

"Best setup, full size, four slots — and never the fifth.
If the grid is full, the next idea waits."

ALWAYS

  • ✓ Take the highest-ranked qualifying setup
  • ✓ Size in fixed ₹10L or ₹5L blocks
  • ✓ Cap aggregate margin at ₹40L
  • ✓ Use half-size for lower-rank or weaker bases
  • ✓ Honor stops on borrowed capital

NEVER

  • ✕ Add a 5th concurrent position
  • ✕ Exceed ₹40L total margin
  • ✕ Use sizes outside the ₹10L / ₹5L grid
  • ✕ Take a lower-rank setup when a higher-rank one is live
  • ✕ Average down on a losing margin position
