2 · Entry timing — when to actually fire

Standard entry window Normal env

Algo fires at 09:31 when the slot is empty and the ATR re-entry gate is clear. Operator decision:

SignalAction
Current premium decaying (LTP < previous 5-min close)Allow entry at the size set by the forecast scorecard.
Premium flat / rising on first 5-min candleHold off; wait one more candle for confirmation.
Forecast strong move kills the tradeSkip the day — no entry.
Penny / illiquid morning Hard-penny env

When ATM premium is so thin that 1 tick is 5 – 10% of premium, the algo’s 100% SL is one bad print away. Override the default 09:31 entry:

RuleWhy
Delay entry beyond 09:31Let the first 5-min candle print; pennies need price discovery.
Enter only after the first spikeBuy yourself a known top to fade — premium needs a high to decay from.
Wait for ATM premium to fall below the day’s openConfirms the decay regime; entering on a rising curve in pennies = instant SL.

Manual-close decision rule

1 amber: note, do nothing.

2 ambers OR 1 red: close 50% of position and pull the other 50% to the algo’s native rails.

2 reds OR VIX +5% intraday: flat the book manually — do not wait for the ₹3,750 × M strategy SL.

4 · Things the algo can’t protect you from

Trend day with normal candles

ATR brake won’t fire — bodies stay below 1.5× ATR. Losing leg chains 100% SLs one restrike at a time. Your RSI(1H) + market-alignment check is the only real defence.

Pinning day, no drift

Strike-drift gate never lifts → 50% TP can’t fire. Premium decays beneath your feet but the algo holds. Either accept the slower decay-to-close, or close manually at 60 – 70% decay around 14:30.

Hard-penny morning

Algo will short ₹15 – ₹30 ATM with the same 100% SL. One bad print = full leg loss. Delay entry, fade first spike, or skip.

Mid-day VIX repricing

A +5% VIX move on a quiet tape will inflate both legs without a single 1.5× ATR candle — the ATR brake stays silent. Watch VIX explicitly in the 15-min loop.
