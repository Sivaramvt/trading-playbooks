Daily Iron Condor (IC-14)

DIC-14 Daily Iron Condor

Professional Trading Manual — Version 1.0

01 Strategy Core

  • Time: Short-dated (1-DTE). Sell Far OTM in the last 30 mins (2:45–3:15 PM).
  • Objective: Generate consistent daily theta income.
  • Instrument: NIFTY Weekly Options (1-DTE).
  • Nature: Market-neutral, Short-Delta, Defined-Risk.

02 Desk Philosophy

  • 1. Risk-First Trading: No trade is worth uncontrolled tail risk.
  • 2. Defined Risk: Wings are mandatory; naked positions prohibited.
  • 3. Consistency: Modest daily profit → large annual compounding.
  • 4. Mechanical Execution: Rules > intuition. Execute the plan.

03 Regime Exclusion

AVOID trading the DIC-14 on days with major known volatility events:

  • Budget Day & RBI Policy Meetings
  • Election Results
  • Major Geopolitical Events
  • US CPI / FOMC (Optional Filter)

Applicable Regimes: VIX 11–25. Avoid VIX backwardation > 1%.

04 Trade Construction & Entry

4.1 Strike Selection (1-DTE)

Leg Delta Target Distance
Sell Call 14Δ 350–450 pts OTM
Sell Put 14Δ 350–450 pts OTM
Buy Wings N/A 300–350 pts from short

Target Credit: Minimum ₹40. Ideal: ₹45–₹75.

5. Entry Rules (Mechanical)

  • Time Window: 2:45 PM – 3:15 PM.

    Maximizes overnight theta and captures late-day IV elevation.

  • Order Type: Single combined Iron Condor order.

    NEVER manually leg into the position.

  • Sizing Cap: Max Loss per IC ≤ 0.35% of capital.

    Max total portfolio exposure ≤ 1.0% of capital.

06 Exit & Risk Management

6.1 Profit Booking

Window: 9:20 AM – 9:35 AM

  • Target: 40–50% of credit captured.
  • Hard Exit: Time exit at 9:35 AM if target not met.
  • Exit immediately on IV crush profit.

6.2 Stop-Loss Triggers (Mechanical)

Rule: ALWAYS exit the ENTIRE IC. NEVER leg out.

  • A. Side-Specific SL: If one side reaches 1.3 × Credit (e.g., option price is 1.3x the full credit received).
  • B. Overall IC SL: Max absolute loss for the IC is 2 × Credit.
  • C. Hard Daily Stop: Stop new IC trading if total daily loss > 0.7% of capital.

7. Risk Control Summary

Tail Protection: Wings limit Max Loss. Gamma Control: 14Δ strikes keep position safe overnight. Gap Management: Max loss capped regardless of gap size.

    • If SL hit: Exit entire IC.
