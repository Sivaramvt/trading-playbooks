Not in v101 (planned in P1 JSON): restrike cap, post–premium-SL cooldown, daily leg-SL circuit breaker. Until promoted, those gaps stay on you via §4–5.

Three reds that always stand you down

Event calendar red — FOMC, RBI, CPI, election result, budget, oil/Israel headline. Theta on these days is a coin flip; tail dominates expected value. Skip, regardless of how green the other inputs are.

Yesterday hit -₹3,750 × M — force a 0-size cool-off day. Sizing back into a losing streak is the most common sustainability failure on short-premium books.

Penny morning red (§1 #6) — multiplier 0 at 09:30. Do not rely on delayed entry; v101 has no penny filter on entry.

Modifier that cuts the band one notch

Margin already > 30% used by other strategies — cap at the Reduced band regardless of forecast (20 mult / ₹50 L). Keep span headroom for intraday SPAN spikes.

Penny amber (§1 #6) also drops one band and defers 09:31 — see §3.

Worked example. Forecast: VIX green, alignment green, decay green, event green, prior-day amber, penny green → 4 G + 1 A → Tactical band: 30 multipliers. Penny amber instead → Reduced (20 mult) and no 09:31 entry.

The forecast (§1) and band (§2) tell you whether to trade and how big. 4 · The 6 lead indicators — what to watch every 15 minutes

Post-entry, run this scorecard every 15 minutes. v101’s RSI + structure check blocks bad re-entries after 11:00 — it does not flatten open legs. Two ambers or one red → manual close ahead of 2× SL. Between 15:15 and 15:23, per-leg exits are off — only the universal exit remains active.

Manual-close decision rule

1 amber: note, do nothing.

2 ambers OR 1 red: close 50% of position and pull the other 50% to the algo’s native rails.

2 reds OR VIX +5% intraday: flat the book manually — do not wait for the ₹3,750 × M strategy SL.

After 15:15: v101 per-leg TP/SL conditions stop evaluating. If still exposed, flatten manually or rely on 15:23 universal exit / strategy SL.

5 · Things v101 can’t protect you from

Trend day with normal candles

Entry gate ≠ open-position defence. v101’s RSI + structure filter only runs when the leg slot is empty (new entry / restrike). It does not cut a leg already in trade.

09:31–11:00: morning bypass — no RSI check on entry. Slow grind up in small bodies (< 1.5× ATR) → ATR brake silent, 2× SL fires late. Each SL can restrike immediately (no post-SL cooldown, no restrike cap).

After 11:00: losing-side re-entry usually blocked — but damage may already be done. While still in, only 2× SL or an ATR spike exits you. §4 Trend + Momentum red → flatten; don’t wait for the chain.

Pinning day, no drift

Drift-gated TP never lifts → 50% TP can’t fire while spot stays in the entry 50-pt bucket. Premium decays beneath your feet but the algo holds. Either accept decay-to-close, or close manually at 60 – 70% decay around 14:30.

Hard-penny morning

Scored in §1 #6 before 09:30. v101 has no penny filter — it will short thin ATM with the same 2× SL. Red → multiplier 0; amber → defer per §3; never let the algo auto-enter at 09:31 on a red penny score.

Instant re-entry after premium SL

Cooldown exists only after ATR exit, not after 2× premium SL. Worst window: 09:31–11:00 (morning bypass + instant restrike). After 11:00 the entry gate may block the next restrike — but §4 #3 red is still the early warning on the first leg.

Mid-day VIX repricing

A +5% VIX move on a quiet tape will inflate both legs without a single 1.5× ATR candle — the ATR brake stays silent. Watch VIX explicitly in the 15-min loop.

15:15 – 15:23 exit gap

Per-leg exits cut off at 15:15; universal square-off is 15:23. Eight minutes with no per-leg TP/SL logic active. Reduce size into the close or manual-flat if indicators are amber.
