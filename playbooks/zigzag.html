Who decides what

Algo owns: ATM strike picking, the 50% TP / 2× SL on each leg, the 5-min ATR shock brake, the 3-calm-candle re-entry gate, the 15:23 hard square-off, and the ₹3,750 × multiplier strategy SL.

Two reds that always stand you down

Event calendar red — FOMC, RBI, CPI, election result, budget, oil/Israel headline. Theta on these days is a coin flip; tail dominates expected value. Skip, regardless of how green the other inputs are.

Yesterday hit -₹3,750 × M — force a 0-size cool-off day. Sizing back into a losing streak is the most common sustainability failure on short-premium books.

Two modifiers that cut the band one notch

Hard-penny morning (ATM straddle ≤ ₹100 combined OR ≤ 0.30% of spot) — one tier lower than the table suggests, OR delay entry per §3 rules.

3 · Entry timing — when to actually fire

The forecast (§1) and band (§2) tell you whether to trade and how big. Before pulling the trigger at 09:31, run a 30-second lead-indicator pre-check on the five indicators that exist at the open — VIX, Trend, Momentum, Price volatility, and ATM premium decay. Then apply the env-specific rules below.

Pre-entry action rule

All 5 green: fire at the band set in §2.

1 amber, rest green: drop one band (e.g. Full → Tactical) and proceed.

2 ambers, or 1 red: defer entry; re-check at 09:45. Use that window to verify Indicators 4 (first-candle ATR ratio) and 5 (premium decay direction).

2 reds, or VIX red, or first-candle ATR red, or premium-decay red: stand down for the day — regardless of what §2 said.

Manual-close decision rule

1 amber: note, do nothing.

2 ambers OR 1 red: close 50% of position and pull the other 50% to the algo’s native rails.

2 reds OR VIX +5% intraday: flat the book manually — do not wait for the ₹3,750 × M strategy SL.

5 · Things the algo can’t protect you from

Trend day with normal candles

ATR brake won’t fire — bodies stay below 1.5× ATR. Losing leg chains 100% SLs one restrike at a time. Your RSI(1H) + market-alignment check is the only real defence.

Pinning day, no drift

Strike-drift gate never lifts → 50% TP can’t fire. Premium decays beneath your feet but the algo holds. Either accept the slower decay-to-close, or close manually at 60 – 70% decay around 14:30.

Hard-penny morning

Algo will short ₹15 – ₹30 ATM with the same 100% SL. One bad print = full leg loss. Delay entry, fade first spike, or skip.

Mid-day VIX repricing

A +5% VIX move on a quiet tape will inflate both legs without a single 1.5× ATR candle — the ATR brake stays silent. Watch VIX explicitly in the 15-min loop.
