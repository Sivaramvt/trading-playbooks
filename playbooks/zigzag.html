Pre-09:30 Forecast Scorecard

Green · Size Up

Range / mean-revert day, VIX < 13 and falling, mixed markets, quiet calendar, calm book.

Amber · Size Down

Mixed signal, VIX 13–16, two of three markets aligned, soft data print, mild drawdown.

Red · Stand Down

Trend/event day, VIX > 16 or +5%, all markets aligned, tier-1 event, prior SL hit.

Four Hazards Algo Cannot Protect From

Trend Day, Normal Candles

ATR brake stays silent while losing leg chains restrikes one SL at a time.

Pinning Day, No Drift

Strike-drift gate never lifts; manually harvest 60–70% decay near 14:30 if needed.

Hard-Penny Morning

₹15–₹30 premium makes one bad print equal full leg loss.

Mid-Day VIX Repricing

A +5% VIX move can inflate both legs without triggering the ATR brake.
